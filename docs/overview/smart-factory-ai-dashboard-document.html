01 Idea Overview

One platform for the whole factory

Smart Factory AI Dashboard applies AI across warehouse management, production line monitoring, safety alerts, and workforce allocation. It helps the factory operate faster and more accurately with less manual paperwork — while supporting managers in making data-driven decisions.

02 System Objectives

What the system is designed to do

1

Real-time monitoring

Monitor factory operations continuously across every line and area.

2

Warehouse optimization

Support goods management and optimize warehouse space usage.

3

Anomaly detection

Detect abnormalities during production before they escalate.

4

Safety warnings

Warn about workplace safety risks in hazardous areas.

5

Productivity analysis

Analyze the productivity of each individual production line.

6

Workforce planning

Recommend schedules, overtime plans, and workforce allocation.

7

Less paperwork

Reduce administrative overhead through smart electronic forms that route and track themselves.

03 Target Users

Built for five kinds of factory people

Factory managers

Monitor overall operations, output, safety, and work schedules from one control hub.

Warehouse managers

Check item locations, warehouse status, and AI storage recommendations.

Production managers

Track line performance, receive fault alerts, and coordinate production.

Employees

Submit forms, receive notifications, and view their work assignments.

Safety teams

Monitor hazardous areas and handle incident alerts as they arise.

04 Detailed Features

Ten modules, one connected system

Each module simulates a real factory capability with AI in a supporting role — monitoring, analyzing, and recommending rather than replacing people.

4.1

Factory Overview Dashboard

MVP
Functions
  • Active, stopped & maintenance line counts
  • Today's output vs target
  • Current alert count
  • Warehouse status & low stock
  • Employees working by shift
  • Productivity charts + high-risk areas
Purpose: Help users see overall factory performance and make faster decisions.
4.2

AI-Powered Warehouse Management

MVP
Functions
  • Track item locations by code or area
  • Fast location search
  • Recommend storage areas
  • Wrong-location alerts
  • Low inventory / overload warnings
  • Rearrangement suggestions + history
Purpose: Reduce item search time, minimize mistakes, and help warehouse staff work more accurately.
4.3

Production Line Monitoring

MVP
Functions
  • List all production lines
  • Status: normal, slow, faulty, paused
  • Output over time
  • Below-efficiency alerts
  • Detect downtime, defects, delays
  • Compare lines + store history
Purpose: Help managers quickly identify which line is facing issues and respond in time.
4.4

AI Camera & Incident Alerts

Functions
  • Display camera feeds of key areas
  • Detect restricted entry / crowding / obstacles
  • Immediate alerts on detection
  • Severity: low / medium / high
  • Store snapshots & event logs
  • Incident statistics by day/week/month
Purpose: Improve automated monitoring and reduce total reliance on human observation.
4.5

Workplace Safety Management

MVP
Functions
  • Factory map with dangerous zones
  • Mark areas with active alerts
  • Warn on restricted-area entry
  • Record incidents + timestamps
  • Early-warning suggestions from history
  • Reports on frequent-risk areas
Purpose: Support the company in improving workplace safety and reducing factory accidents.
4.6

AI-Based Productivity Analysis

Functions
  • Analyze productivity per line
  • Actual vs planned output
  • Performance trends over time
  • Identify most efficient periods
  • Flag high defect-rate lines
  • Suggest efficiency improvements
Purpose: Help managers not only see the numbers but understand trends and possible causes.
4.7

Work Schedule & Workforce Allocation

MVP

Smart Notifications & Alerts

06 Main Workflow

From login to decision, in seven steps

A manager logs into the system.

The manager views the overview dashboard.

They select a module — warehouse, production, safety, or workforce.

The system displays real-time or simulated data.

AI analyzes the data and provides alerts or recommendations.

The manager confirms, adjusts, or acts on the suggestions.

Results are stored to generate reports and improve future recommendations.

07 Key Highlights

Why this concept stands out

One unified platform

Integrates multiple factory operations — warehouse, production, safety, workforce — into a single platform.

AI supports, not replaces

AI assists monitoring, analysis, and recommendations — people stay in control of every decision.

Strong practical value

Addresses common, real factory challenges rather than abstract or theoretical problems.

Easy to demonstrate

Presentable with dashboards and simulated data — no need to build a fully functional AI system.
